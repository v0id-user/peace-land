---
title: "Faded"
date: 1870-06-28
slug: faded
---

I don't know what happened exactly.
Something clicked inside of me.
I'm trying to resist it.

Because I still have you.
I still want you.
I'm begging you to try with me.

But you started to fade.
I started to not care.
I don't know why it happened so fast.

But you faded.
I don't know how to feel about it.
I don't know if it is right, if it is wrong.
But I promise that this is not what I want.
