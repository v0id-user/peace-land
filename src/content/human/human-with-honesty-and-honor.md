---
title: "Human with Honesty and Honor"
date: 1924-04-11
slug: human-with-honesty-and-honor
---

For a long time, I played the silence game. But it was never me. I thought it was strength. It was weakness. Let cowards play it.

Now I wrestle out loud. I fight out loud. I say my points out loud. I even state my weaknesses out loud in front of everybody. Because I have nothing to hide. Because everything I show is growth.

For those who prefer the silence game, stay weak. Stay down. Stay in your own delusions that silence is a power game. It never was. It never will be.

Never silence. Build fortune.
Never silence. Build legacy.
Never silence. Make a human. Look a human.
Never silence. Make honor.
Never silence. Make honesty.
